Welcome to the Extinction Rebellion Earth Fast

On 22 April 2021 the UK Earth Fast begins

The Earth Fast starts on Earth Day, 22 April 2021, and will involve many rebels and other activists voluntarily going without food for a period of time, from a day and up to a week or two (or longer), using this hunger strike as a form of non-violent direct action. The target of this hunger strike is to force the government to implement a citizens' assembly and to put the global climate emergency at the top of its agenda.

A large number of those participating within the UK will be longer-term fasters who will be fasting from a hunger camp based in London and within view of the Houses of Parliament, where the physical camp will serve as an unrelenting presence to heighten awareness and significantly increasing the visibility of the action.
