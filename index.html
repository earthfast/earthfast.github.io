Welcome to the Extinction Rebellion Earth Fast

On 22 April 2021 the UK Earth Fast begins

The global Earth Fast starts on Earth Day, 22 April 2021, and will involve many rebels and other activists voluntarily going without food for a day, others for a week and others will fast until our demands are met, using this fast as a form of non-violent direct action. The target of this fast is to force the UK government to establish and be led by a citizens' assembly, putting the global climate and ecological emergency at the top of its agenda.

A large number of those participating within the UK will be longer-term fasters who will be fasting from a hunger camp based in London, within view of the Houses of Parliament, where the physical camp will serve as an unrelenting presence to heighten awareness and significantly increasing the visibility of the action.

The following countries will be participating in the global Earth Fast: Algeria, Australia, Bangladesh, Canada, Democratic Republic of Congo, Denmark, Egypt, France, Gambia, Germany, India, Italy, Kenya, Malawi, Mali, New Zealand, Nigeria, Spain, Sri Lanka, Sweden, Tunisia, Uganda, United Kingdom, United States and Zambia.
